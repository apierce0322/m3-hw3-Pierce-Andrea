About SWV

SWV (Sisters With Voices) is an American R&B vocal trio from New York City, formed in 1988. The group is composed of Cheryl Gamble (Coko), Tamara Johnson (Taj), and Leanne Lyons (Lelee). They rose to fame in the 1990s with a string of hit singles and albums that helped define the sound of contemporary R&B.

Winnings

Debut Album: It's About Time (1992)

This multi-platinum album featured hits like "Weak," "I'm So Into You," and "Right Here/Human Nature."

Impact and Legacy

SWV's soulful harmonies and street-savvy style influenced a generation of female R&B groups. They were one of the best-selling girl groups of the 1990s and continue to perform together, maintaining a strong legacy in the music industry.
